Detection\n====================================================\n\n2021-06-23\n\nIf an app installed on your LDPlayer instantly crashes after you launch it, or an alert of \"This device has been rooted\" pops up to block your access for the app, try the troubleshooting steps below. The possible reason for the issue is that your emulator has enabled root permission and this conflicts with the launching of your apps.\n\n**Disable Root Permission on LDPlayer**\n---------------------------------------\n\nThe root permission on LDPlayer is disabled by default. However, if you have enabled the feature before and forgot to close it afterward, you can now disable the root permission in the **Settings** of LDPlayer.\n\nOpen your emulator setting panel and go to \"Other settings\" >>> Disable root permission >>> Save the setting and restart the emulator\n\n![Fix App Instant Crash Due to Rooted Device Detection](https://res.ldrescdn.com/faq/images/en/51e5601b-f773-4667-b9b8-f9ed667c9e50.jpg?x-oss-process=image/format,webp/quality,Q_100/resize,w_563)\n\nIf you're using LDPlayer 3, the setting panel looks like a little bit different. But you can find it on \"Basic\" tab and you just need to uncheck the setting of \"Enable root permission\".\n\n![Disable Root Permission on LDPlayer 3](https://res.ldrescdn.com/faq/images/en/363087e7-9f36-4dca-b556-6b1719ae1789.webp?x-oss-process=image/format,webp/quality,Q_100/resize,w_680)\n\nBy disabling the root permission on LDPlayer, you can access the game without crash or access issue for some specific games, such as [Epic Seven](https://www.ldplayer.net/games/epic-seven-on-pc.html)\n, [Captain Tsubasa: Dream Team,](https://www.ldplayer.net/games/captain-tsubasa-dream-team-on-pc.html)\n etc.\n\n**An Example from Arknights**\n-----------------------------\n\nBefore disabling root permission, a dialog box will pop up when you try to enter **Arknights: Application Protection**. Or the game just crashes itself instantly after you open it.\n\nAfter disabling root permission on LDPlayer, you can play the game without any further issue.\n\n![Fix App Instant Crash Due to Rooted Device Detection](https://res.ldrescdn.com/faq/images/en/15916c77-cc89-4e99-a0c4-738a961d156c.jpg?x-oss-process=image/format,webp/quality,Q_100/resize,w_800)\n\nIf your emulator has already disabled the root permission but your app still crashes, it might be other problems.
