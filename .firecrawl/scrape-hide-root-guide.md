|\n\nRepository files navigation\n---------------------------\n\n*   [README](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#)\n    \n\nMore items\n\nGuide: Hiding Root from Banking and Other Apps (2025)\n=====================================================\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#guide-hiding-root-from-banking-and-other-apps-2025)\n\nThis guide provides a step-by-step process for hiding root access from sensitive applications like banking apps (e.g., GCash) that use root detection and Google's Play Integrity API.\n\n> **⚠️ Disclaimer: A Note on Root Solution, Compatibility, and Timeliness**\n> \n> The steps and modules in this guide are written specifically for **Magisk** and were successfully tested on a device running **Android 15**. The information is current as of **July 8, 2025**.\n> \n> While the core principles of hiding root may translate to other solutions like **KernelSU**, this guide has **not** been tested on those platforms. They have their own unique mechanisms and module ecosystems, so you will likely need to find alternative modules and adapt the steps accordingly.\n> \n> Furthermore, bypassing root detection is a constant \"cat-and-mouse game.\" Success is never guaranteed and can depend on your specific device, software version, and security patch. Please be aware that these methods may become obsolete over time.\n\n* * *\n\n🛠️ Step 1: Initial Setup & Cleanup\n-----------------------------------\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#%EF%B8%8F-step-1-initial-setup--cleanup)\n\nBefore installing new modules, it's crucial to start with a clean slate.\n\n1.  **Update your Magisk App**: Ensure you are on the latest stable version of Magisk.\n2.  **Hide the Magisk App**:\n    *   Open Magisk -> Tap the **Settings** icon (⚙️) in the top right.\n    *   Under \"App\", tap **Hide the Magisk app**.\n    *   Give it a new name (e.g., \"Settings\" or \"Manager\") and let it reinstall.\n3.  **Disable Conflicting Modules**:\n    *   Open Magisk -> Go to the **Modules** tab.\n    *   **Disable all existing modules** to avoid conflicts. You can re-enable non-essential ones later, one by one, if needed.\n4.  **Clear App Data**:\n    *   Go to your phone's Settings -> Apps.\n    *   Find and clear storage/data for the following apps:\n        *   The app you want to hide root from (e.g., `GCash`).\n        *   `Google Play Services`\n        *   `Google Play Store`\n\n* * *\n\n📦 Step 2: Install Required Modules & Apps\n------------------------------------------\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#-step-2-install-required-modules--apps)\n\nDownload and install the following tools. You can find them on GitHub or XDA-Developers.\n\n*   **Modules (Install via Magisk)**:\n    \n    *   [Play Integrity Fork](https://github.com/osm0sis/PlayIntegrityFork/releases)\n        \n    *   [Shamiko](https://github.com/LSPosed/LSPosed.github.io/releases)\n        \n    *   [Zygisk - LSPosed](https://github.com/JingMatrix/LSPosed/releases)\n        \n    *   [Zygisk Assistant](https://github.com/snake-4/Zygisk-Assistant/releases)\n        \n    *   [Zygisk Next](https://github.com/Dr-TSNG/ZygiskNext/releases)\n        \n*   **Apps (Install as normal APKs)**:\n    \n    *   [HideMyApplist](https://github.com/Dr-TSNG/Hide-My-Applist/releases)\n        \n    *   [Play Integrity API Checker](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=gr.nikolasspyr.integritycheck)\n        \n\nAfter installing all Magisk modules, **reboot your device**.\n\n* * *\n\n⚙️ Step 3: Configure Magisk Settings\n------------------------------------\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#%EF%B8%8F-step-3-configure-magisk-settings)\n\nWith the required modules installed, configure Magisk for optimal hiding.\n\n1.  Open Magisk -> Tap the **Settings** icon (⚙️).\n2.  **Disable Built-in Zygisk**: Ensure the `Zygisk` toggle is **OFF**. We are using Shamiko to manage this.\n3.  **Disable DenyList Enforcement**: Ensure the `Enforce DenyList` toggle is **OFF**. Shamiko uses its own blocklist logic.\n4.  **Configure DenyList**:\n    *   Tap on `Configure DenyList`.\n    *   Find your target app (e.g., `GCash`) and check the box next to it. Tap on the app name to expand it and **check all sub-processes**.\n    *   Find `Google Play Services`. **Check the main box only**. Do _not_ expand and check its sub-processes.\n    *   Find `Google Play Store`. **Check the main box only**. Do _not_ expand and check its sub-processes.\n5.  **Verify Shamiko Mode**:\n    *   It should report that Shamiko is in **Blacklist mode**.\n\n* * *\n\n🔒 Step 4: Configure LSPosed & HideMyApplist\n--------------------------------------------\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#-step-4-configure-lsposed--hidemyapplist)\n\nThis step prevents apps from detecting other root-related apps on your device.\n\n1.  **Enable LSPosed**:\n    *   You should see a notification from LSPosed after rebooting. If not, open the **LSPosed** app from your app drawer.\n    *   Go to the **Modules** section.\n    *   Enable **HideMyApplist** by turning on its toggle.\n2.  **Configure HideMyApplist**:\n    *   Open the **HideMyApplist** app.\n    *   Go to **Template Manage**.\n    *   Create a new **Blacklist Template**. Name it something memorable like `Hide Root Apps`.\n    *   In this new template, add only the apps that have root access (Magisk, LSPosed, your file explorer, etc.) to the invisible apps.\n    *   Also inside this new template, find your target app (e.g., `GCash`) and put it to the applied apps list.\n    *   Go back to App manage and select your target app (e.g., `GCash`) and `Enable hide`.\n\n* * *\n\n✅ Step 5: Verify Play Integrity\n-------------------------------\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#-step-5-verify-play-integrity)\n\nNow, let's check if the setup passes Google's integrity check.\n\n1.  Open the **Play Integrity Fork** app.\n2.  Press the **Check** button at the bottom.\n3.  The app will run a check. You are looking for the result `MEETS_BASIC_INTEGRITY`.\n4.  **If the check fails (shows X):**\n    *   In the Play Integrity Fork app, tap **Action**.\n    *   Delete Play Integrity API Check Storage and open the app.\n    *   Press **Check** again.\n    *   Repeat this \"Delete Storage\" and \"Check\" process a few times. It can sometimes take several attempts to get a passing result.\n5.  Once you successfully see `MEETS_BASIC_INTEGRITY`, you are ready for the final step.\n\n* * *\n\n🚀 Step 6: Final Steps\n----------------------\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#-step-6-final-steps)\n\n1.  **Restart your device** one last time.\n2.  After rebooting, open your target app (e.g., `GCash`). It should now launch without detecting root.\n\n❓ Troubleshooting\n-----------------\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#-troubleshooting)\n\n*   **Still Detected?** Be sure that before doing this guide, you have cleared the storage for your targetted app, play store, and play services. Double-check that all conflicting Magisk modules are disabled, Shamiko is in blacklist mode, and you have correctly configured the DenyList and HideMyApplist.\n*   **What about `MEETS_DEVICE_INTEGRITY`?** Passing `MEETS_DEVICE_INTEGRITY` is much harder and often requires a specific device fingerprint. For most apps, `MEETS_BASIC_INTEGRITY` is sufficient.\n*   **SafetyNet vs. Play Integrity:** SafetyNet is deprecated. Play Integrity is the new standard for app-level security checks.\n\n* * *\n\n❤️ Acknowledgments\n------------------\n\n[](https://github.com/hyowonbernabe/Hide-Root-Guide#%EF%B8%8F-acknowledgments)\n\nThis guide is a compilation of community knowledge.
