  \n*   International merchant classification\n    \n*   Real-time payment verification\n    \n*   Automatic retries for failed charges\n    \n\nBecause OpenAI operates globally, payments are OpenAI\n--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------\n\nPayment declines can happen for several reasons:\n\n*   Card not enabled for international transactions\n    \n*   Prepaid BIN flagged as high-risk\n    \n*   Insufficient balance during renewal\n    \n*   Multiple rapid retry attempts triggering fraud filters\n    \n*   Mismatch between billing address and card profile\n    \n*   Bank restrictions on AI-related merchants\n    \n\nEven valid debit cards can fail if issuing banks apply strict online service filters.\n\nRecurring billing environments are especially sensitive to authorization stability.\n\nRequirements for OpenAI-Compatible Virtual Cards\n------------------------------------------------\n\nTo improve approval rates, a virtual card should:\n\n*   Operate on Visa or Mastercard networks\n    \n*   Support recurring subscription billing\n    \n*   Allow international USD transactions\n    \n*   Provide stable BIN classification\n    \n*   Maintain sufficient balance before billing cycles\n    \n*   Offer real-time transaction visibility\n    \n\nVirtual cards structured for SaaS and digital services tend to perform better than local-only debit cards.\n\nCommon Payment Errors and How to Fix Them\n-----------------------------------------\n\nHere are typical OpenAI payment issues and solutions:\n\n### Error: Payment Declined\n\n*   Confirm sufficient balance\n    \n*   Avoid repeated rapid retries\n    \n*   Check if international transactions are enabled\n    \n\n### Error: Card Not Supported\n\n*   Replace with a card that supports recurring billing\n    \n*   Avoid certain prepaid or local-only debit cards\n    \n\n### Error: Billing [Authorization Failed](https://buvei.com/blog/credit-card-transactions-authorization-vs-capture/)\n\n*   Ensure card details are entered correctly\n    \n*   Verify billing address consistency\n    \n*   Reissue the card if necessary\n    \n\nBecause OpenAI retries failed payments automatically, unresolved issues may lead to temporary
